GENERAL INFO


: Win the Game by reaching the treasure chest

: Be careful and don't touch the spikes



MOVEMENT


Use тнв and тна to move right or left and SPACE to jump.

You can glide along walls by jumping against them.

You are able to perform Double Jumps if you start jumping off the ground.



LEVEL EDITOR


You are able to create and play your own Levels !!!

With Grid Size, Width and Heigth you can define these Level Properties.

Use the tiles displayed in the upper left corner to just draw your Level.

Each Level needs a 'Player'-Start and 'TreasureChest'

If you want to delete some tiles just use the erase button.

After you finished your Level just download it and to play it use 'Durchsuchen' in the Game Screen.

By using 'Durchsuchen' in the Level Editor Section and 'Clear/Redraw'
you can reload a Level and work on it again.
